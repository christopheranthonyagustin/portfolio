# Data Warehousing, Analytics & Customer Reporting Platform

## Overview

The Data Warehousing, Analytics & Customer Reporting Platform was developed to provide centralized reporting, operational analytics, and business intelligence capabilities across the company's Order Management System (OMS), Warehouse Management System (WMS), e-commerce operations, logistics platforms, and supply chain ecosystem.

The platform consolidated operational data from multiple business systems, online marketplaces, logistics providers, enterprise applications, and customer platforms to support data-driven decision-making, performance monitoring, operational visibility, and customer reporting requirements.

The solution enabled internal stakeholders and external customers to access business insights through automated reporting, interactive dashboards, and analytics-driven performance monitoring.
