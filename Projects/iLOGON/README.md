# End-to-End E-Commerce & Supply Chain Integration Platform

## Overview

The End-to-End E-Commerce & Supply Chain Integration Platform is an enterprise integration ecosystem centered around the company's Order Management System (OMS), serving as a middleware layer between e-commerce marketplaces, ERP systems, warehouse management systems, logistics providers, customer service applications, and enterprise customer platforms.

The platform was designed to support multi-channel commerce operations by consolidating orders, inventory, fulfillment processes, shipment tracking, customer communications, and operational reporting into a unified integration framework.

The solution enabled organizations to automate business processes, improve operational visibility, reduce manual intervention, and support scalable e-commerce and supply chain operations.
