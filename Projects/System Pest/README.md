# Cloud-Based Field Service & Mobile Workforce Management Platform

## Overview

The Cloud-Based Field Service & Mobile Workforce Management Platform is an enterprise solution designed to support service-oriented organizations through centralized work order management, service scheduling, technician dispatch, customer management, mobile workforce coordination, and operational reporting.

The platform enables field service organizations to efficiently manage service requests, assign technicians, track work progress, monitor operational performance, and improve service delivery across distributed field operations.

The solution was deployed on Microsoft Azure infrastructure to provide scalability, availability, and operational continuity while supporting both office-based operations teams and mobile field personnel.
