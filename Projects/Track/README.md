# Fleet & Transport Management System

## Overview

The Fleet & Transport Management System is an enterprise platform designed to support transportation, logistics, field service, and fleet operations through real-time vehicle monitoring, GPS tracking, telematics integration, dispatch management, route optimization, and operational reporting.

The platform evolved from an initial fleet tracking solution into a comprehensive transportation management ecosystem supporting vehicle operations, driver management, dispatch workflows, route planning, resource utilization, and operational decision-making.

Over multiple years of continuous enhancement, the platform supported various transportation and logistics use cases across Singapore and regional markets.
